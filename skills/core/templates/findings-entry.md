### Status Derivation Rules
- **preliminary**: single evidence entry
- **supported**: 2+ consistent entries (all supports/refines, no contradicts)
- **robust**: 3+ entries across different datasets or projects
- **contradicted**: ANY contradicts entry exists (takes precedence regardless of support count)

### Direction Definitions
- **supports**: evidence consistent with the claim
- **contradicts**: evidence inconsistent with the claim
- **refines**: evidence that narrows or qualifies the claim
